History

White water rafting has a rich history that spans centuries and involves various cultures. Here's a brief overview of the history of white water rafting. White water rafting traces its roots back to ancient times when people started using rafts for transportation and trade along rivers with challenging rapids. Rafts were initially practical tools for navigating turbulent waters. In the 19th century, explorers and adventurers began using rafts for recreational purposes, exploring rivers and their rapids for the thrill of the experience. The concept of using rafts for leisure laid the foundation for the development of modern white water rafting.
